Minimum System Requirements

CPU Minimum: Intel 386 DX
CPU Recommended: Intel Pentium P5

RAM Minimum: 1MB
RAM Recommended: 2MB

Display: 640x480 - 1280x720
Graphics Adapter Minimum: CGA
Graphics Adapter Recommended: VGA

Mainboard has to support EDD (Enhanced Disk Drive Services)

Recommended Disk Size: 16MB
Maximum Disk Size Supported: 2GB
Maximum amount of disks: 5 (2 floppies and 3 hard disks)

This system does not support the i8086, even tough it its
written in 16-Bit assembly.
